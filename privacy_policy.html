Privacy Policy for Upload to ImageKit

Last Updated: 10 November 2024

1. Introduction

Upload to ImageKit (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is a Google Chrome extension that allows users to upload images from any webpage directly to their ImageKit account. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

  • ImageKit API Key: We collect the ImageKit API key that you provide within the extension settings. This key is necessary to authenticate and authorize uploads to your ImageKit account.
  • Images: When you choose to upload an image from any web page, the extension accesses the image data to facilitate the upload to your ImageKit account.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • ImageKit API Key: Used solely for authenticating and authorizing image uploads to your ImageKit account.
  • Images: Accessed only when you initiate an upload; the images are transmitted directly to ImageKit via HTTPS and are not stored or processed by us beyond this purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your ImageKit API key is stored locally on your device within the Chrome extension’s storage and is not transmitted to any servers controlled by us.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Third-Party Services

  • ImageKit: Your interactions with ImageKit, including the upload of images, are governed by ImageKit’s Privacy Policy. We do not control ImageKit’s data practices and encourage you to review their policies.

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Information is only shared with ImageKit as necessary to perform the uploads you initiate.

7. User Choices and Controls

  • API Key Management: You can update or delete your ImageKit API key at any time through the extension’s settings.
  • Uninstallation: You can uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all stored data from your Chrome browser.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the extension, and the “Last Updated” date at the top will reflect the date of the latest revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
